Gas Station Example

If you went to the gas station to fill your tank with 20 gallons of gasoline and the attendant said that the first 10 gallons were on sale, would you say...

“YES, OF COURSE I WOULD LIKE HALF OF MY GAS AT A DISCOUNT’

OR

“If I can't secure a discount on all 20 gallons, I'd rather pay full price for the entire quantity. I'm disappointed that the discount isn't as substantial as I had hoped. For me, it's either a significant discount or I'm willing to pay the full amount."
